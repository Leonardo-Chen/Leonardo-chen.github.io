Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

This website is designed to collect only the information needed to operate the portfolio and understand which work is being viewed.

  • Local browser storage used to remember display preferences such as dark mode and the privacy notice
  • Basic visit analytics collected by a self-hosted Umami instance, such as visited page, referrer, device type, browser family, and approximate country or region
  • Aggregated engagement events that help estimate how long visitors actively stay on a page

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this limited information only for:

  • Providing and maintaining the website
  • Understanding which projects and pages are most useful to visitors
  • Improving content structure, navigation, and overall user experience

3. Cookies and Local Storage

This site stores a small amount of information in your browser to support basic functionality. This storage is used to:

  • Store your dark/light mode preference
  • Remember whether you dismissed the privacy notice

The analytics setup is intended to be privacy-friendly and cookieless. If the analytics configuration changes in the future, this policy should be updated accordingly.

4. Analytics Hosting

Analytics for this portfolio are processed through a self-hosted Umami instance. The analytics dashboard is used only by the site owner to review aggregated traffic data such as page popularity, traffic sources, and approximate visitor geography.

The system is configured to avoid collecting unnecessary personal information. Depending on the hosting setup, network information may be processed briefly to derive approximate location and traffic metadata before being minimized or discarded.

5. Third-Party Services

This website may load resources from the following services:

  • Google Fonts
  • Font Awesome
  • YouTube no-cookie embeds on pages that contain embedded video

6. Your Rights

If you are covered by applicable privacy law, you may have the right to:

  • Request information about the data processing described here
  • Request deletion of personal information where applicable
  • Object to or limit non-essential analytics processing where required by law
