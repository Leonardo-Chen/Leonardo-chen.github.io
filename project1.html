Future Travel

Future Travel is a speculative installation that uses AI and body movement to generate travel destinations on a projected map. Inspired by the fusion of physical motion and digital imagination, it allows users to explore non-existent cities through interactive gestures.

Technologies Used

  • Arduino + Motion Sensors
  • AI Image Generation (e.g. Stable Diffusion)
  • Projection Mapping
