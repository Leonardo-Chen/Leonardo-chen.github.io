I'm
Zheng Chen

An Interdisciplinary designer and researcher crafting interactive systems that blend AI, spatial experience, and everything magic.

Selected Projects

A collection of my recent work in interaction, intelligent systems, mobility, and care-oriented products.

Tubi

Tubi is an interactive installation that blurs the line between reality and fiction. Inspired by AI-generated playgrounds, it reacts to visitors' movements with sounds and changing visuals, creating a surreal experience where the physical and digital worlds merge.

Remi

A conversational companion for people with early-stage dementia, designed to support daily independence and family communication through multimodal interactions.

Air Purifier

A reproduction of the NS Nanotech Air Purifier.

Sony A7r IV

A 3D modeling project of Sony A7r IV using Fusion 360.

Sony TC 150 Reproduction

A speculative design installation that uses AI to generate travel destinations based on your movement and touch.

Robotion

An interactive device which helps autistic children remember to train their emotional intelligence by associating situations and emotions.

Rover.ai

Rover.AI is an intelligent travel app that transforms user-generated content into personalized, real- time travel experiences. Designed for modern explorers, it helps you discover, plan, and adapt your journey with ease — all powered by AI.

Web design

A speculative design installation that uses AI to generate travel destinations based on your movement and touch.

Pixel Race

A motion-controlled racing game on a custom LED matrix device, using accelerometer input and arcade-inspired visuals to create a playful handheld experience.

Paintings

Selected paintings and visual studies from studio practice. A gallery exploring colour, composition, and personal themes — click through for full-screen views.

Wuxi — Audiovisual Short

Short audiovisual piece introducing Wuxi to an Italian audience—collage-style city portrait with Chinese visual motifs, inspired by João Pombeiro’s Chicago for Lance Skiiiwalker.
